Who We Are

The International Relations – Model United Nations Club is an amazing platform for Creighton students to learn, discuss and engage in diplomatic affairs and international politics. On top of promoting global awareness on the Creighton campus, the Club sends a delegation of students to two Model UN conferences: the American Model UN conference in the fall semester and the Harvard World Model UN conference in the spring. The next World Model UN conference will be held in Rome, Italy. In the past, members have travelled to Seoul, Brussels, Melbourne, and Singapore, among others.

The International Relations – Model United Nations Club welcomes students of all academic disciplines.

What we do

  • Discuss a wide range of challenging topics from disarmament and international security to economic and social empowerment.
  • Research on the economic, social, and cultural policies of various countries
  • Learn about the different roles and mechanics of the UN bodies
  • Explore ethnic cuisines across the greater Omaha area and beyond
  • Host prominent speakers in the fields of politics and international relations
